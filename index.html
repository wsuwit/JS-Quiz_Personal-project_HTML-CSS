About us

Introduction, we are Thailand Codecamp students (Codecamp#9) studying about web development.
Infact, most of us have come from different fields of study and worked in different industies (only a few graduated from Computer Science).
But we all have the same goal "to become a programmer".
Struggling, we have learned that for a beginner to Fullstack JavaScript world, it is quite hard and plenty of subjects to learn.
Through the difficulty we met, the only key to give you is "just keep practicing".
As mentioned above, our suffering through the intense lessons due to our unprepared knowledge for web development is a good lesson learn for beginner who wish to study in a Codecamp.
The subjects you might need to learn before joining a web development Codecamp: UX-UI, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Git, React, Node.JS, Database, BasicSecurity, Redux.js, TDD, Webpack, Docker, SDLC+Document, Agile, CleanCode, Style-guide and Creating projects.
Here with this application, is part of our web development project. We wish this little JS based quizzes can guide you to JS world.
